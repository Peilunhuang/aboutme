As an artist I have created photomontages to reveal a personal vision about the nature of children, animals, and their interactions. These images illustrate the fleeting moods that can't be captured by a traditional camera or seen by the naked eye. This website offers an opportunity to view the range of my work created over the past decade. My hope is that you will explore and enjoy a different type of photography.
